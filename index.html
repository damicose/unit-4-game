CRYSTAL COLLECTOR

You will be shown a random number at the start of the game: this is your target score.

When you click on a crystal it will add a specific, hidden amount of points to your total score.

You win if your total score matches the target score. You lose if your score goes above the random number.

After a win or loss the game will reset with new randomly-generated numbers.
